Sorcerer

[In-Game Description] - Sorcerers summon and control weather phenomenon: hurling lightning bolts and creating electrified fields, summoning tornadoes and impenetrable fog, and calling upon Daedric forces to summon Storm Atronachs and magical armor.

Sorcerers generally are used as a DPS. The majority of damage dealing spells are lightning based. The sorcerer also has the ability to summon pets which can summon pets which can fight along side them. The Daedric Summoning line offers damage and stuns. Dark Magic offers support by removing debuffs, restoring health and magicka, in addition to stuns and knockdowns. Storm Calling offers damage and AoE skills.
