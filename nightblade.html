Nightblade

[In-Game Description] - Nightblades are adventurers and opportunists with a gift for getting in and out of trouble. Relying variously on stealth, blades, and speed, Nightblades thrive on conflict and misfortune, trusting to their luck and cunning to survive.

The Nightblade features both stealth and health-draining skills, in addition to high burst damage attacks. Nightblades are almost always going to be used as DPS. In PVP, they're often used to take out high-priority targets, such as enemy healers. If the enemy team's healers are dead, the remaining foes are that much easier to eliminate. The Assassination line offers damage and debuffs. Shadow does AoE damage in addition to debuffing. Siphoning has DoT as well as buffs.
