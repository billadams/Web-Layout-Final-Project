Character Races

The Elder Scrolls Online has nine playable character races: Nord, Redguard, Breton, Imperial, Dunmer, Altmer, Bosmer, Orsimer, Khajiit, and Argonian.

Each race has a specific allegiance they adhere to: The Ebonheart Pact, The Aldmeri Dominion, or The Daggerfall Covenant.
