Character Classes

The Elder Scrolls Online has four main character classes: Dragonknight, Sorcerer, Nightblade, and Templar.

Choosing a class determines which class-specific skill lines are available. Certain character classes are more well-suited to specific roles than others, however any class can fulfill any role. The three major roles are tank, healer, and DPS. Generally the Dragonknight is a tank, the templar a healer, and either a sorcerer or nightblade fill the DPS role. As stated earlier, these are just general guidelines for class usage.
