Exploring Tamriel

Welcome to the Elder Scrolls Outpost. Here you can find the latest game updates and news, along with character building tips and links to various other resources I find useful and relevant for maximizing your enjoyment while exploring Tamriel.

ESO is a huge game and it's easy to get lost in all the various aspects of character creation, gear selection, and just what in the world you should start doing. The answer to the latter is, anything you want! ESO is as open-ended as they come.
