Dragonknight

[In-Game Description] - These skillful masters-at-arms use the ancient Akaviri martial arts tradition of battle-spirit, and wield fearsome magic that pounds, shatters and physically alters the world around them.

Dragonknight's generally are used as tanks in ESO. They do fairly well as mages thanks to the Ardent Flame skill line which does fire damage. They also do well in PVP situations since the Draconic Power skill line provides self-healing. Finally, the Earthen Heart skill line has lots of utility for defense and group support.
